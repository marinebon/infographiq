Guide to Infographiq

Chapter 9 Organization of Infographiq components

The components of Infographiq are distributed across several GitHub repositories. The organization of these repositories are described in this section of the guide, via the table below.

In the table, you can see that the rows are divided into Builders and Instances:

  • Builders: GitHub repositories that contain the building blocks of the Infographiq functionality.

  • Instances: GitHub repositories where Infographiq is being used to display scientific data.

Also in the table below, you can see that the repositories are divided across three columns:

  • MBON: The Marine Biodiversity Observation Network is a growing global initiative composed of regional networks of scientists, resource managers, and end-users working to integrate data from existing long-term programs to improve our understanding of changes and connections between marine biodiversity and ecosystem functions.

  • NOAA-ONMS: NOAA’s Office of National Marine Sanctuaries serves as the trustee for a network of underwater parks encompassing more than 600,000 square miles of marine and Great Lakes waters. The network includes a system of 15 national marine sanctuaries and Papahānaumokuākea and Rose Atoll marine national monuments.

  • NOAA-IEA: Through an integrated science approach, the NOAA Integrated Ecosystem Assessment program balances the needs of nature and society for current and future generations, supporting diverse marine resource management objectives in an ecosystem context.
